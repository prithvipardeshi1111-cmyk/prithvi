About Balaji Pure Veg

A beloved vegetarian restaurant in the heart of Panvel, serving authentic South & North Indian food with warmth and tradition.

Our Story

Balaji Pure Veg has been a cornerstone of the Panvel food community, located conveniently opposite the ST Bus Stand in Navi Mumbai. We take pride in serving 100% pure vegetarian food made from the freshest ingredients.

From crispy South Indian dosas and soft idlis to hearty North Indian thalis and rich paneer curries — our menu is a celebration of India's diverse vegetarian cuisine. Every dish is prepared with care, love, and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast before your bus, enjoying a leisurely lunch thali, or dining with family — Balaji Pure Veg is always ready to serve you with warmth.
